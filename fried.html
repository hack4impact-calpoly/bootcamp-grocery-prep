Fried Rice

Dinner Recipes Pasta Recipes Rice Recipes

Time 20-30 minutes

Fast and simple but tastes delicious

The beauty about fried rice is that you can add whatever you want. So you can always expand on this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Day old cooked rice
  • Note: rice must be atleast a day old. Do not use fresh cooked rice
  • 2-3 eggs
  • Green onions
  • Canola oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Add canola oil to a wok or high walled pan
  2. Add the eggs and break the yokes
  3. While the eggs are still cooking, add the day old rice and mix well
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste and some soy sauce
  5. Add green onions and mix
