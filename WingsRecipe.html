Easy Chicken Wings Recipe With Ingredients and Instructions



Ingredients
  • 2-1/2 pounds chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce

Instructions
  1. Cut wings into 3 section, discard wing tip
  2. Place in greased 13"x9" baking dish
  3. Combing BBQ, honey, and soy sauce, pour over wings
  4. Bake, uncovered, at 350F for 50-60 minutes
