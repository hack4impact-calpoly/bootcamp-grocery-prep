Mango Chicken Curry

One of my go to and favorite dishes!

Ingredients

  • 15 ounce jar of Patak's Mango Curry simmer sauce or any brand you prefer
  • 1 lb of salted diced chicken breast
  • 2 cups of rice

Instructions

  1. Heat a little cooking oil in a deep sided frying pan
  2. Add diced chicken breast on medium heat and make sure all the chunks are tourching the pan
  3. Using a rice cooker cook the rice according to your rice cooker (typically 1 cup of water for 2 cups of rice)
  4. Keep the diced chicken still until once side is fully browned
  5. Immeditaly flip the chicken chunks over and add the jar of sauce to the pan
  6. Simmer for 15 minutes stirring occasionally
  7. Serve over rice and add Chutney if you really want a new yummy twist

Chef's Advice: Add lots of salt! If the meal doesn't taste quite right add more salt! And don't forget to salt the raw chicken!
