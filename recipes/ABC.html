ABC Omelette

A savory omelette that is nutritious and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 4 strips of bacon
  • 1 handful of cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Heat pan and cook bacon until desired doneness
  2. Use bacon grease left over as substitute for oil
  3. Whisk 3 eggs until homogenous
  4. Pour mixed eggs into pan and wait until it sets
  5. Once able to flip, flip
  6. Put cooked bacon and cheddar chesse on the omelette and wait until you can fold
  7. Fold the omelette and put onto a clean plate
  8. Cut avocado and lay nicely on top of your finished omelette
  9. Enjoy!
