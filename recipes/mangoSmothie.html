Mango Smothie

Nothing like a nice mango smothie on a hot day!

Ingredients

Servings

1
  • .375 cup reduced fat milk
  • 1 oz nonfat greek yogurt
  • .375 tsp vanilla extract
  • .75 cup sliced mangos
  • .5 cup of ice

Instructions

  1. Blend it all together until its nice and smooth
  2. Enjoy!
