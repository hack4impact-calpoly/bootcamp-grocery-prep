Caramel Latte

Caramel and caffine.

Ingrediends

Servings

1
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 1 teaspoon of caramel sauce

Instructions

  1. Steam the milk
  2. Add caramel sauce to the cup first
  3. Pour the milk into the cup with the espresso shot
  4. Enjoy!
