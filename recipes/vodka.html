Creamy Tomato Vodka Sauce

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons of Olive oil
  • 1 can of unpeeled whole tomatoes
  • 1 whole basil with stem
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • Red pepper flakes to taste
  • 1lb of penne pasta
  • 3 tablespoons of vodka
  • salt to taste
  • Optional: grated parmesan or pecorino romano cheese to tase
Note: This recipe can feed 3-4. If you are cooking for one use 1 cup of penne and save the sauce for later. General rule of thumb is one cup of pasta per person.

Instructions

  1. Add the olive oil to a pot and turn it on medium
  2. Peel the garlic cloves and slice thinly
  3. Add the tomatoes to blender and pulse until mostly smooth
  4. Add the galic and basil into the pan
  5. Before the garlic browns, turn off the heat and deglaze with the vodka
  6. Turn the heat back on and burn off the alcohol
  7. Add the tomatoes and red pepper flakes to the pot
  8. Reduce the tomato sauce until the it leaves a path when a spoon is dragged across the bottom of the pan. About 40 minutes
  9. Add some of the vodka sauce to the pasta and mix
  10. Optional: Top with grated parmesan or pecorino romano cheese
