Frappucino

Blended flavor and ice

Ingrediends

Servings

1
  • 1 cup of ice
  • 1 teaspoon of flavoring
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of frappucino base
  • (Optional) coffee
  • (Optional) whipped cream

Instructions

  1. Fill .3 of your cup with milk
  2. Add coffee if desired
  3. Pour everything into the blender
  4. Add ice
  5. Add flavoring
  6. Add frappucino base
  7. Blend it all together
  8. Pour into your cup
  9. Add whipped cream as desired
  10. Enjoy!
