Queso

Ingredients

  • Block of cheese
  • 2 jalepenos
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 lime
  • 1/3 cup cilantro
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 Tbsp butter

Instructions

  1. Chop up the onions, jalepenos and garlic
  2. Melt the butter on medium heat in a pan
  3. Sautee the onions, jalepenos, and garlic in the butter until fragrant
  4. Slowly add in the cheese, stirring until melted
  5. Add the rest of the ingredients
