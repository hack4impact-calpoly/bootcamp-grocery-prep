Guacamole

Both a great snack, and something fun to pair with

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe avocadoes
  • 4 ripe roma tomatoes
  • 1 small yellow onion, chopped
  • 2 limes
  • 2 jalepeno peppers
  • 1/3 cup fresh cilantro

Instructions

  1. Chop up the tomatoes, jalepenos, onion, and cilantro.
  2. Smash avocadoes.
  3. Mix ingredients together.
  4. Eat!
