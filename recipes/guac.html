Guacamole

An easy to make dip for chips!

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 0.125 diced red onion
  • 0.5 lime
  • 2 avocados
  • 1 tsp. of finely diced jalapenos
  • 1 handful of diced cilantro
  • 0.5 diced tomatoe

Instructions

  1. Have chilled mixing bowl ready
  2. Soak diced onions in the lime juice you squeeze
  3. Get the flesh of 2 avocados and mash them in a bowl
  4. Put cilantro, onions and lime juice, tomatoes, and jalapenos in the guacamole
  5. Mix thoroughly but gently
  6. Serve with some chips and enjoy!
