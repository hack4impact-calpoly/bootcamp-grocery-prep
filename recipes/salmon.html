King Salmon

Make sure you have a little cooking experience under your belt before you try this one.

Servings 1

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of fresh king salmon with skin on
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon olive oil
  • .5 teaspoon of sweet smoked paprika
  • 12 fresh diced strawberries (optional, but it is my preferred choice)
  • 1 diced mango (exclude if not in season
  • .25 diced avacado
  • 6 fresh mint leaves cut into ribbons
  • 1 tablespoon of limoncello
  • 1 dash of cayenne pepper

Instructions

  1. Dice the fruit and avocado
  2. Add the mint and Limoncello and gently mix together and sprinkle with cayenne pepper
  3. For the salmon: Heat the oven to Hi-Broil
  4. Rinse the salmon, pat dry with paper towels, cut into two portions
  5. Brush the salmon with lemon olive oil, sprinkle with sweet smoked paprika
  6. Place on a rimmed baking sheet, skin side down
  7. Place the baking sheet on the rack, 4-5 inches from the broiler and cook for about 6 minutes, depending on thickness
  8. Remove from oven and press gently on the fish with a fork, if the flesh parts (flakes) it is done. Do not overcook.
  9. Serve salmon and salsa together

Chef's Advice: Salmon is best when prepared to medium doneness - not completely cooked through
