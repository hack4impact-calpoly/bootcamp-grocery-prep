BLT Sandwich

A simple but delicious sandwich!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • 6 leaves of lettuce
  • 4 strips of bacon
  • 4 slices of tomatoe

Instructions

  1. Heat pan and cook bacon until desired doneness
  2. Toast bread in toaster
  3. Put your lettuce on the bottom slice of bread
  4. Then tomoatoes
  5. Then bacon
  6. Then last piece of bread on top
  7. Put onto a clean plate
  8. Enjoy!
