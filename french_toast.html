French Toast

Delicious french toast. Enjoy anytime, anywhere.

Servings

5

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of cinnamon bread
  • 5 eggs
  • 0.5 cup(s) milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla liquid
  • 1 stick(s) of butter

Instructions

  1. Scrabmle eggs into a large enough, flat-bottom dish to put the bread slices in
  2. Pour a little bit of milk in there
    • I just use whatever type of milk
  3. Add a tiny bit of vanilla
  4. Dip both sides of bread slice in the mix
  5. Heat pan on med/low with butter
  6. Put the bread on the pan and cook both sides until it's ready
  7. Continue this process until you have as many french toast pieces as you want
