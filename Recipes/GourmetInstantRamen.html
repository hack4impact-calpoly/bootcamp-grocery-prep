Gourmet Instant Ramen

This is the perfect midnight snack that doesn't take much prep at all

This is my favorite snack late on a weekend night

Ingredients:

  • 1 Pack of Instant Ramen of your choosing
  • 2 Green Onions
  • Kamaboko
  • Togarashi

Directions:

  1. Follow cooking instructions of the Ramen
  2. Slice green onions
  3. Slice Kamaboko (3-4 slices)
  4. Add green onions and sliced Kamaboko to Ramen
  5. add togarashi to ideal spice levels
