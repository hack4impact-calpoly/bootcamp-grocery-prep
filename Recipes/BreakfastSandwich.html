Breakfast Sandwich

This is my take on Einstiens Bacon and Cheddar Breakfast Sandwich

This is my favorite meal in the morning given I have all the ingredients

Ingredients:

  • Bagel of choice
  • 2 Strips of Bacon
  • Slice of Cheddar
  • One Egg

Directions:

  1. Cook the 2 slices of Bacon
  2. cook the egg over hard
  3. after the egg has been flipped place slice of cheddar on top of egg
  4. slice bagel and lay the egg-cheddar combo and top with bacon to complete sandwich
