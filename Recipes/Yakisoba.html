Yakisoba

This is a very easy to make japanese noodle dish given you have the instant package

This is one of my favorite dishes to make for all my roomates when we are struggling to find something to eat

Ingredients:

  • Instant Yakisoba Noodles
  • 1/4 cup of chopped carrots
  • 1/4 cup of chopped Napa Cabbage
  • 1/4 cup of chopped spam

Directions:

  1. Cook noodles following directions on package
  2. strian noodles
  3. add vegetables and spam to pan without noodles
  4. add noodles and spice package
  5. serve
