Fried Rice

This is a very basic yet delicious recipe for fried rice

Its gonna sound strage but fried rice is better when you use day old rice

Servings:

1

Ingredients:

  • Soy Sauce to taste
  • 2 Cups of Rice (preferably day old)
  • 2 Green Onions
  • .25 Cups of Sweet Onions
  • 2 Egg
  • 3 Slices of Bacon
  • .5 Cups of Chopped Baby Carrots

Directions:

  1. Cut Bacon into small bits and cook till cooked
  2. Place rice into wok and mix in Bacon, Sweet Onion
  3. pour soy sauce into rice until the color of the rice changes into a light brown
  4. move rice to the side of the wok and cook the egg in the same wok
  5. once egg is cooked mix it all together
  6. add green onions salt and pepper
