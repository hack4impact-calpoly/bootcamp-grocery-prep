Grilled Cheese

An easy and delicious meal for days that you are too busy to cook

Servings
 0

Ingredients

  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar

Instructions

  1. Spread 1 tablespoon butter on one side of each slice of bread
  2. In a skillet over medium heat, melt one tablespoon of butter
  3. Place the bread butter side down on the skillet and top each slice of bread with 1/2 cup of cheddar cheese
  4. Cook until bread is golden and cheese is melted
  5. Flip one slice of bread on top of the other
  6. Enjoy!
