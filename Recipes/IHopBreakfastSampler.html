Breakfast Sandwich

This is my favorite dish from IHop the traditional diner breakfast

Simple yet delicious

Servings:

1

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup of Frozen Hash Browns
  • 2 Strips of Bacon
  • 2 Link Sausages
  • 2 Slices of Canadian Bacon
  • 2 Eggs

Directions:

  1. Preheat a pan on the stove
  2. lay the ham, bacon and sauage
  3. cook till the meat has a desired texture
  4. cook eggs as you want. I like my eggs over hard
  5. in new pan, lightly oil then pan fry the frozen hash browns
