Fried Rice

An easy and simple meal for days when you are too lazy to cook!

Servings
 0

Ingredients

  • 0.5 tablespoon oil
  • 1 cup cold rice
  • 0.5 cup veggies of your choice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Add oil to pan and scramble the eggs
  2. Add the veggies to the pan
  3. Add the cold rice to the pan and stir
  4. Once rice is heated through, stir in soy sauce
  5. Plate and enjoy!
