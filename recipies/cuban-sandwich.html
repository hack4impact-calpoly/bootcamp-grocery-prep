Cuban Sandwich

This sandwich is full of flavor and easy to make

1

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • 2 Slices of Deli Sliced Ham
  • 0.25 Pound of Pulled Pork
  • 1 Slice of Swiss Cheese
  • 3 Pickles
  • 1 Tsp Musturd
  • 0.1 Stick of Butter

Instructions

  1. Butter bread.
  2. Assemble ingredients on sandwich.
  3. Toast sandwich in panini press for 3 minutes.
