Fried Rice

This lunchtime meal is easy to make and always tastes great

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Carrots
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Cook rice.
  2. Cut carrots and begin steaming them for a couple minutes.
  3. Prepare scrambled eggs
  4. Add butter to skillet
  5. Add cooked rice to skillet
  6. Cook rice over high heat for 3-4 minutes, continually adding butter to avoid sticking.
