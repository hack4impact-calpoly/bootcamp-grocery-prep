Breakfast Sandwich

A yummy way to start the day.

Ingredients

  • English Muffins
  • 2 Egg
  • Sausage Patty
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Beat eggs until blended
  2. Cook Eggs in skillet for 2 minutes
  3. Cook Sausage for 4 minutes, adding cheese after flipping
  4. Toast english muffins and assemble
