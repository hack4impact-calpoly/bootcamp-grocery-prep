Bratwurst

This is a great dinner thats relatively easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Bratwurst
  • Buns
  • Sauerkraut
  • Musturd
  • 1 Tbl Butter

Instructions

  1. Put a tablespoon of butter in the pan and heat for a minute.
  2. Once pan is hot cook bratwurst over medium heat for 10 minutes, turning occasionally.
  3. Add sauerkraut to pan and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  4. Put brotwurst and sauerkraut in bun and top with musturd.
