Acai Bowl

This is a great healthy lunch option for a sunny day!

Ingredients

  • 2 Samazon Acai Packets
  • 1/4 Cup Milk
  • 1/4 Cup Orange Juice
  • 1 Bannana
  • Granola
  • Honey
  • Almond Butter

Instructions

  1. Thaw acai packets for 30 seconds under warm water.
  2. Put acai packets, milk, orange juice, and 1/2 bannana in the blender.
  3. Blend until consistancy is smooth.
  4. Pour out of blender.
  5. Top with 1/2 bannana, granola, almond butter, and honey.
