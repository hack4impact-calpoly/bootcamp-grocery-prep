Acai Bowl

This is a great healthy lunch option for a sunny day!

1

Ingredients

  • 2 Samazon Acai Packets
  • 0.25 Cup Milk
  • 0.25 Cup Orange Juice
  • 1 Bannana
  • 0.5 Cup Granola
  • 1 Tbsp Honey
  • 2 Tbsp Almond Butter

Instructions

  1. Thaw acai packets for 30 seconds under warm water.
  2. Put acai packets, milk, orange juice, and 0.5 bannana in the blender.
  3. Blend until consistancy is smooth.
  4. Pour out of blender.
  5. Top with 0.5 bannana, granola, almond butter, and honey.
