Blueberry Pancakes

Cook these fluffy and flavorful pancakes for a great breakfast!

Ingredients

  • Pancake Mix
  • 2 Cups Water
  • Blueberries

Instructions

  1. Mix pancake mix with water.
  2. Pour pancake mix on griddle.
  3. Top mix with blueberries after pouring.
  4. Cook pancakes until light brown.
