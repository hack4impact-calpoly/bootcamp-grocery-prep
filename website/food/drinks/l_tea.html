Tea

You know when Naruto goes Nine-Tail? Yeah this tea is crazier than that.

Ingredients

  • 9 Tea Cups
  • 3 Ninroot
  • 1 Cat
  • 1 Stimpack
  • 2 Chaos Emeralds
  • 1 oz of 'Cool' Water
  • 3 oz of Sunny D
  • 3 oz of Skooma

Instructions

  1. Arrange the tea cups in a circle.
  2. Pet the cat 3 times.
  3. Grind the Ninroot until it sparkles.
  4. Put the Ninroot in the Sunny D.
  5. Pet the cat 3 more times.
  6. Inject the stimpack into the 'Cool' Water.
  7. Break the 2 chaos emeralds.
  8. Harness the energy.
  9. Mix 4 times more.
  10. Throw away the Skooma.
  11. Pour an even amount in the 9 cups.

ONLY DRINK ONE
