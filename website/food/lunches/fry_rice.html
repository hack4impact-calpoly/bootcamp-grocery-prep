Fish Fried Rice

Its awesome, and totally better than any other rice. I know that's a bold claim, and it's probably wrong.

Ingredients

  • 10 Tilapia
  • 1 tblspn Paprika
  • 1 tblspn Lemon Pepper
  • 1 tblspn Tumeric
  • 2 tblspn Soy Sauce
  • 4 cups Basmati Rice

Instructions

  1. Spice the fish. A lot.
  2. Cook the rice.
  3. Cook the fish.
  4. Mix the fish and the rice.
  5. Also put tumeric.
  6. Mix like 4 times more.
  7. Put soy sauce in.
  8. Mix 4 times more.
