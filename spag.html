Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Dinner Recipes Pasta Recipes Rice Recipes

Time: 15 to 20 minutes

Simple Light Pasta Dish

There are a lot of variations of this dish, usually using parsley and or lemon, however this is how I like it

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • 3-4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 packet of Spaghetti
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Salt
Note this recipe can feed 3-4. It is okay to make the full batch and keep in the fridge for up to a week.

Instructions

  1. Fill a pot with water, salt generously and turn on the heat
  2. Once boiling, add the spaghetti and cook until aldente
  3. Peel and chop the garlic into thin slices
  4. In a high walled pan, put the olive oil in and turn the heat on low/medium
  5. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes to the pan
  6. Note: do not brown the galic you want it to keep its color. Turn down the heat if need be
  7. Once the pasta is aldente, use tongs and put the spaghetti in the high walled pan
