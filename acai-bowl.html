Acai Bowl

Typically enjoyed after breaking a personal record at the recreation center, after finishing that difficult route at the climbing wall, or even after going months without intense excercise, the acai bowl is essential to the Cal Poly student's diet. Loaded with micronutrients and inspired by ShakeSmart's campus- and nationally-acclaimed acai bowls, this dish is an accessible, healthy, and refreshing option, especially for breakfast.

Servings

1

Ingredients

Acai Blend

  • 1 packet (100 grams) frozen acai berry packet
  • 0.5 cup (118g) 100% apple juice from concentrate
  • 1 small frozen banana
  • 0.5 cup frozen strawberries
  • 0.5 cup frozen blueberries

Toppings

  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 handful vanilla almond granola, or a similar store bought/homemade granola
  • 0.5 cup fresh fruit, such as fresh strawberries, raspberries, blueberries, and banana
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon coconut

Instructions

  1. Add the acai berry packet, apple juice, banana, strawberries, and blueberries to a high powered blender. Blend until smooth. Pour into a large bowl.
  2. Top the bowl with as much (or as little) of your favorites such as granola, fresh (thinly sliced) fruit, chia seeds, and coconut! I recommend drizzling on about a tablespoon of honey over everything. Enjoy immediately.
