Jasmine Green Tea

Just like green tea, but with the sweet jasmine aroma.

Ingrediends

  • 1/2 spoon of green tea leaves
  • 1/2 spoon of jasmine tea leaves
  • 1 cup of water

Instructions

  1. Boil your water
  2. Pour the water over your tea leaves and into your cup
  3. Enjoy!
