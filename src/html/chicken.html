Chicken and Corn

A convenient, quick and easy recipe for lunch.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 c. extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 pt. cherry tomatoes
  • 2 c. frozen corn
  • 4 oz. Velveeta, cubed
  • Optional: other cheeses
  • Optional: fresh basil

Instructions

  1. Season chicken breasts and sear on skillet until golden, let rest.
  2. Add olive oil to skillet, add garlic, put on low to medium heat.
  3. Add cherry tomatoes and corn, let cook, stir sparingly.
  4. Meanwhile, slice chicken breasts on a diagonal.
  5. Top tomato and corn mixture with sliced chicken and cheese. Let melt about 10 minutes then serve.
