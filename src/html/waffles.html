Waffles and Eggs

For when you're late for class.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2-3 Eggo frozen waffles
  • Optional: maple syrup

Instructions

  1. Heat up your waffles.
  2. Make scrambled eggs, or really any kind of eggs.
  3. Serve on one dish, add syrups to taste.
  4. Remember to season your eggs for a gourmet experience.
