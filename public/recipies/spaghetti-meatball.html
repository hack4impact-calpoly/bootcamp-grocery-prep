Spaghetti & Meatballs

Its hard to go wrong with this Italian classic!

1

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of Spaghetti
  • 24 Oz of Pasta Sauce
  • 8 Meatballs

Instructions

  1. Heat water over stove until boiling.
  2. Begin heating meatballs if frozen.
  3. Cook spaghetti as directed.
  4. Heat sauce over stove top on low, putting in meatballs once cooked.
  5. Once spaghetti is ready, remove all ingredients and serve.
