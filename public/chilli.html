Easy Chilli

Meat and chilli? What could go wrong

Serving Size:

1

Ingredients

  • 1 cup - ground beef
  • McCormick Season or Cajun Spice as needed
  • 2 tbs - Oil
  • 1 whole - Bell Pepper
  • 1 can - Tomato Sauce
  • 1 cup - Chilli Powder

Instructions

  1. Season beef
  2. Brown beef
  3. Prinkle Chilli Powder till dark red
  4. Cook till ready, add water as needed
