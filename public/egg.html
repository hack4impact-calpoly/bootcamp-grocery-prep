Szechwan Eggplant

Spicy and delicious

Serving Size:

1

Ingredients

  • 1eggplant
  • 2 tbs oil
  • 2 tbs soy sauce
  • 2 tbs vinegar
  • 1 tbs sugar
  • 3 tbs ginger minced
  • 3 tbs garlic minced
  • 1cup ground pork
  • 1 tbs red peppers

Instructions

  1. Cut eggplant into 2-inch strips
  2. In a wok or skillet, head oil and fry eggplant till soft. Remove oil and set aside
  3. Pour out oil, fry meat and stir in eggplant
  4. Combine, stir, enjoy!
