Spain

Paella Valencia

Ingredients

  • 3/4 pound peeled and deveined large shrimp
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 chopped tomato
  • 2/3 white wine
  • 1 Arborio rice or other short-grain rice

Instructions

  1. Heat all ingredients on medium-high heat
  2. Sprinkle chicken in salt and black pepper and then bring to boil
  3. Serve
