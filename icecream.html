Ice Cream

It's as cliche as is sounds, ice cream for deserts, perfect for pairing with StEAk!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 6 large egg yolks

Instructions

  1. Simmer heavy cream, milk, sugar, and salt
  2. Whisk yolks in separate bowls
  3. Slowly pour hot liquid to the yolks
  4. Strain and cool the mixture
  5. Chill it overnight and serve
