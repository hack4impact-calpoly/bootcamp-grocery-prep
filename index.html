Welcome to Taste of Campus

Just because you're living off-campus shouldn't mean that you need to pay astronomical prices for the finest meals that Cal Poly has to offer, convince a freshman to spot you with their PolyCard, or abstain from eating campus food entirely. Taste of Campus is a free, one-stop meal preparation resource for Cal Poly students living off-campus or whose meal schedules fail to align with the operating hours at many university dining venues.
