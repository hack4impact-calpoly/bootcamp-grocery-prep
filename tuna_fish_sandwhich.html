Tuna Fish Salad Sandwhich

Super quick, super yummy!

Ingredients

  • 2 6 ounce cans of tuna. Drain unless specficed by can
  • 1/2 cup of chopped celery
  • 1/4 cup of chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 teaspooon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of pepper
  • 8 slices of bread

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, mix the tuna, celery, onion, mayonnaise, lemon juice, salt and pepper
  2. Spread tuna mixture on 4 bread slices. Top with remaining bread slices

Chef's Advice: Tuna fish salad sandwhichs are hard to mess up, just go with confidence!
