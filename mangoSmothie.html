Mango Smothie

Nothing like a nice mango smothie on a hot day!

Ingrediends

  • 3/8 cup reduced fat milk
  • 1 oz nonfat greek yogurt
  • 3/8 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup sliced mangos
  • 1/2 cup of ice

Instructions

  1. Blend it all together until its nice and smooth
  2. Enjoy!
