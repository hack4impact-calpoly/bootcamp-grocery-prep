Baked Purple Sweet Potato

Sweet, Purple, and Potato!

Ingredients

  • Purple Sweet Potato
  • 2 Tbs Butter
  • salt
  • black pepper

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425F
  2. Wash Potatoes
  3. Stab Potatoes
  4. Bake potatoes on baking sheet until soft
  5. Add butter, seasoning, anything you want!
