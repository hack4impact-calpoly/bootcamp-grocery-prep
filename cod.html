Black Cod

Super quick, super yummy!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of cod per person cut into portions
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Salt raw cod on both sides
  2. Heat pan with cooking oil until it is is shimmering
  3. Put cod in pan with skin side up
  4. Cook until crispy then flip
  5. Add a lid to the pan and cook until done
  6. If desired score each peice of fish three times to prevent curling

Chef's Advice: This is an incredibly tricky dish to get right, so repition and practice is going to be the only sure fire way to nail this.
