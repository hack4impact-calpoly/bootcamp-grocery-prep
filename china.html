Pork Dumplings!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 medium head Napa cabbage
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1 pound of ground pork
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 large eggs whisked
  • 1 package round dumpling, wonton, or gyoza wrappers

Instructions

  1. Slice Cabbage
  2. Squeeze the liquid from cabbage
  3. Combine the cabbage with the rest of the filling ingredients
  4. Arrange dumpling making station
  5. Place tablespoon of filling on each dumpling and wrap with drop of water
  6. Repeat and then cook or freeze dumplings!
