Breakfast Croissantwich

Start your day off right with this very yummy breakfast (or lunch) croissantwich.

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 1 croissant(s)
    • (the ones from Costco are s-rank)
  • 1 egg(s)
  • 2 piece(s) of lettuce
  • 0.5 sliced tomato(es)
  • 0.25 avocado
  • 1 cheese slice(s)
  • 1 pinch(es) of salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Slice croissant open and put cheese on it
  2. Melt cheese over croissant
    • Use toaster oven if possible
    • Else do it on the pan
  3. (option) Sizzle some ham/bacon and put that on the sandwich too
  4. Crack an egg on the pan and fry it
    5. (I recommend overcooking the egg a little so it doesn't run everywhere when you bite into it)
  5. Put cooked egg on croissant slice.
  6. Add decorations as desired
    • sliced tomato
    • lettuce
    • avocado
    • salt / pepper
  7. Close the sandwich
