Hot Chocolate

Nothing beats a nice cup of hot chocolate on a cold day!

Ingrediends

  • 1 pack of hot chocolate poweder
  • 1 cup of milk of your choosing
  • (optional) marshmallows
  • (optional) whipped cream

Instructions

  1. Heat up your cup of milk
  2. Add the hot chocolate powder to your cup
  3. Add mashmallows and whippe cream as desired
