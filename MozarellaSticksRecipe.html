Mozarella Sticks Recipe


Serving Size
4

Ingredients
  • 1.5 cups Italian-style dried breadcrumbs
  • 1.33 cups freshly grated Parmesan
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 (16oz) blocks pasteurized mozarella cut into 4 by 1/2-inch sticks
  • 4 large eggs, beaten to blend
  • 1.5 cups vegetable oil
  • 4 cups Marinara Sauce
Instructions
  1. Stir bread crumbs, Parmesan, and salt in bowl to blend.
  2. Dip cheese in eggs, coat completely.
  3. Coat cheese in bread crump mixture.
  4. Place cheese sticks on baking sheet.
  5. Coat sticks with eggs and breadcrumbs second time.
  6. Cover and freeze for 2 hours - 2 days.
  7. Heat oil in frying pan.
  8. Fry cheese until golden brown, 1 minute per side.
  9. Sprinkle remaining cheese and serve with Marinara.
