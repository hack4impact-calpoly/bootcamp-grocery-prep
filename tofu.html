Spicy Tofu Soup

Perfect for cold or even any weather. Spicy tofu soup.

Servings

4

Ingredients

  • 1 spicy tofu soup kit
    • from any Asian market near you
  • 4 (optional) egg(s)
  • 2 (optional) cup(s) rice
  • extras
    • 1 cup(s) bok choi
    • 0.5 cup(s) mushrooms
    • 1 unit(s) of kimchi

Instructions

  1. Follow instructions on tofu soup package
  2. add decorations while soup is cooking
  3. Drop an egg in to be fancy
  4. Cook rice if that's what you need
