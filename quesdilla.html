Quesdilla

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 2 large tortillas
  • 1 cup of cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil

Instructions

  1. Take out all the ingredients, a pan, a spatula, a knife, and a plate
  2. Put the pan on the stove with medium height
  3. Pour the olive oil in the pan and coat the bottom of the pan
  4. Place one tortilla in the pan
  5. Sprinkle the cheese evenly over the tortilla
  6. Place the other tortilla on top
  7. Cook until the bottom tortilla turns light, golden brown
  8. Flip the quesadilla over
  9. Cook until the bottom tortilla turns light, golden brown
  10. Turn off the stove and slide the tortilla onto a plate
  11. Cut the tortilla into slices to your liking
  12. Optional: Add salsa, hot sauce, guacamole, or sour cream to the side
  13. Enjoy the quesadilla! Perhaps even eat it while watching the sunset!
