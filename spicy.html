Spicy Sausage and Rice

Dinner Recipes Pasta Recipes Rice Recipes

Time: 40-50 minutes

A more heavy but tasty meal

Ingredients

  • 1/2 of a red onion, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, deseeded and chopped
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • paprika
  • 5-6 spicy italian suasages, removed from casing
  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cups of chicken stock
  • 1/2 of a cup of white wine
  • Green onion
  • 1-2 tablespoons of Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Add olive oil to a high walled pan, once hot add the sliced red onion. Cook for 5-6 minutes
  2. Add the garlic and red bell pepper
  3. Take the sausage and tear of small chunks and add it to the pan
  4. Add paprika to the mixture to desired liking
  5. Cook until there suasage is cooked 3/4 of the way through
  6. Add the rice and mix for about 30 seconds to 1 minute
  7. Deglaze the pan with the white wine
  8. Once the alcohol is burned off, add the chicken stock
  9. Simmer for 30-40 minutes or until rice is cooked through
  10. Add sliced green onions as a garnish
