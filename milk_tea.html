Milk Tea

This drink is a delicious alternative to coffee for those who choose a high-caffeine tea, like myself. Perfect for morning, afternoon, late night homework grind...

(boba not included)

Ingredients

  • tea
    • (I highly recommend using black tea, but choose any really)
  • water
  • milk of choice
    • (oat, soy, whole, half&half, etc.)
  • sweetener of choice
    • (Honey or brown sugar recommended)

Instructions

  1. Bring water to boil
  2. Pour water over 2 tea bags (or 2x serving of loose-leaf)
  3. Add sweetener while tea is warm, for better dissolving
  4. Allow to steep while the tea cools
    • can leave it out
    • or put it in frdge
    • or shake some ice in there (will water down the tea)
  5. Add milk
  6. (option) Add ice
