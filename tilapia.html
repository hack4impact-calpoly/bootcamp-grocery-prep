Tilapia

A dish of fish and rice/pasta, because what else would it go with. Can also be enjoyed with your choice of side.

Ingredients

  • tilapia filets
    • I get the frozen ones from Costco
  • seasonings
    • Mrs. Dash
    • dill
    • pink himalayan salt
    • seasame seed oil
    • paprika
  • white wine
    • does not have to be cooking wine, cheap $2 bottle will do
  • rice or pasta
  • optional sides
    • tomato
    • brussel sprouts
    • broccoli
  • butter

Instructions

  1. Cook rice or pasta
  2. Season fish
  3. Heat pan on med/med-high with butter
  4. Cook fish in pan
    • After a couple minutes, pour some wine on the sides / in between fish and let it sizzle
  5. Flip the fish and repeat
  6. Prepare sides as desired
