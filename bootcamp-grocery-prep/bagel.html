Bagel

A great snack that is easy to make when you are feeling a bit lazy



Ingredients

  • 1 bagel
  • Cream cheese of choice

Instructions

  1. Slice bagel in half
  2. Place each half in toaster
  3. Grab cream cheese from fridge as well as a plate and butter knife
  4. Once bagel is done toasting, spread cream cheese along the open side
