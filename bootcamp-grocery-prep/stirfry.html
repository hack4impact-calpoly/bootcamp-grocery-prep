Stir Fry

Delicious stir fry you'll be able to save and enjoy for days



Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1/4 of a sweet or red onion
  • Garlic
  • 1 cup of carrots - sliced
  • 1 cup portobello mushrooms - sliced
  • Soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Add all veggies to a pan with olive oil and cook on medium heat
  2. Add ginger and salt/pepper
  3. Slice and season chicken
  4. Throw in chicken and soy sauce for it all to cook in
  5. Cook until chicken is thoroughly cooked - add spices/soy sauce to taste
  6. Optional: cook/heat up rice
  7. Enjoy!
