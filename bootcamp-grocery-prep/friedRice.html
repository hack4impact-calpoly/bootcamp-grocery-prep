Fried Rice

The best way to use old rice



Ingredients

  • 2 cups old cold rice
  • 1 cup carrots
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 chicken breast
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Soy sauce

Instructions

  1. On a side pan cook carrots in olive oil
  2. Add chicken to this and cook until chicken is thoroughly cooked
  3. Scramble 2 eggs in a large pan and cook in butter
  4. When eggs are well cooked, add all of the rice and stir
  5. Add chicken/carrots, soy sauce, salt, and pepper
  6. Mix well
  7. Throw some chopped green onions on top and enjoy!
