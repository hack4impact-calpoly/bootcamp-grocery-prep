Banana Toast

This tasty toast makes the perfect hunger tamer, whether that be morning breakfast, morning snack, afternoon snack, or late night snack...

Ingredients

  • bread of choice (sliced)
    • (also recommend cinammon bread for extra yum)
  • peanut butter
  • banana
  • honey

Instructions

  1. Toast a slice of bread, nice and crunchy
  2. Spread some pb on that bread
  3. Slice and place banana pieces onto bread
  4. Drizzle some honey on there
