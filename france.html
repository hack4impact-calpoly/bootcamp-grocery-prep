France

Crepes!

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of milk and water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablesppon butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Whisk flour, eggs, water, milk, salt, and butter till smooth
  2. Heat mixture on pan
  3. Cook for 2 minutes and serve!
