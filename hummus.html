Hummus

A simple, easy, and healthy snack that can be paired with chips or veggies!

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2-3 tablespoons of water

Instructions

  1. Drain and rinse chickpeas
  2. Add chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and lemon juice to food processor
  3. Process until you have reached desired consistency
  4. Enjoy with chips or veggies!
