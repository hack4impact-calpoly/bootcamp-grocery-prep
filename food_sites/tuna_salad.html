Tuna Salad

Eat fish and a salad at the same time. It's tuna salad

Servings

2

Ingredients

  • 1 can of tuna
  • 2 handful of spinach

Instructions

  1. Put spinach in a bowl
  2. Add tuna to the top of the spinach

It's really that easy!
