Breakfast for Dinner

For when you're too lazy to really cook, but still want to have a complete meal

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of coffee (mocha
  • 1 Jimmy Dean sausage
  • 1 slice of wheat bread
  • 2 scoops of jam
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the sausage
  2. Cook the eggs
  3. Add salt and pepper to taste
  4. Make the coffee using a Mr. Coffee (Coffee machine)
  5. Toast the bread
  6. Add jam to toast
