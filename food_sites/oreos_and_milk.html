Oreos and Milk

Quite possibly the easiest recipe on the entire site, yet still packs an amazing taste

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 12 oreos
  • 1 glass of milk

Instructions

  1. Dunk oreos in milk
  2. Let them soak for a while
  3. Commence devouring
