Avocado Toast

Embrace your inner millennial with this quirky recipe

Servings

2

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 tbsp of pepper

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread
  2. Spread the insides of the avocado onto the bread
  3. Add pepper
