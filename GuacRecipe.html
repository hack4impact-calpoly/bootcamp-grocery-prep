Easy Guacamole Recipe With Ingredients and Instructions




Serving Size
4

Ingredients
  • 3 avocado, peeled, pitted, mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 plum tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper
Instructions
  1. Mash avocados, lime juice salt. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper. Refrigerate 1 hour
