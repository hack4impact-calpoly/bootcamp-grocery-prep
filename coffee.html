Coffee

To help you wake up in the morning~

Ingrediends

  • 1 cup of filtered water
  • 2 tablespoons of coffee grounds for every eight ounces of water

Instructions

  1. Line the basket of your coffee maker with a filter
  2. Grind coffee beanr to med size
  3. Boil filtered water
  4. Pour water into filter to wet it compleatly, let it drain into your cup and then discard the water in the cup
  5. Measure the ground coffee into the wet filter and pour enough water to wet the ground beans and drain into your cup
  6. Pour in the rest of the water
  7. Enjoy!
