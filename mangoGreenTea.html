Mango Green Tea

Green tea, but with a fruity twist.

Ingrediends

  • 1 spoon of green tea leaves
  • 1/2 spoon of mango puree
  • 1 cup of water

Instructions

  1. Boil your water
  2. Pour the water over your tea leaves and into your cup
  3. Mix the mango puree into your green tea
  4. Enjoy!
