Easy Mac n' Cheese Recipe With Ingredients and Instructions



Ingredients
  • 450g macaroni pasta
  • 450g mature cheddar cheese, sliced
  • 15g butter
  • salt and pepper
  • 340mL evaporated milk

Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 374F
  2. Bring large pot of slightly salted water to boil
  3. Add pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes, drain
  4. Grease 2 liter baking dish. Place quarter of macaroni in bottom, followed by even layer of one-quarter of the cheese slices. Dot with butter and season with salt and pepper. Repeat layering 3 times. Pour milk over top
  5. Bake, uncovered, for 1 hour
