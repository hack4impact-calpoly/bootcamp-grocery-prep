Cereal

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cereal of your choosing
  • 0.25 cup frozen berries
  • 0.5 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Take out all the ingredients, a big bowl, and spoon
  2. Add the cereal and frozen berries to the bowl
  3. Pour the milk over top
  4. Enjoy the cereal! Perhaps even take it outside and eat in the sun!
