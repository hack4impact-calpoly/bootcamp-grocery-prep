Tacos!!

An easy, but yummy, dinner!

Ingredients

  • Diced tomatoes
  • Diced onion
  • Diced lettuce
  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • Taco seasoning
  • Package of hard taco shells
  • Sour cream
  • Grated cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Heat taco shells as directed on package
  2. Apply oil on 10-inch skillet and turn up to medium heat
  3. Cook beef medium heat after skillet is warm for 8 to 10 minutes. Chop into tiny pieces using spatula while cooking. Cook until browned
  4. Add taco seasoning and some water. Cook for a little longer
  5. Put graded chees, ontion, tomatoes, lettuce, and beef onto taco shell. Apply dollop of sour cream and eat!

Chef's Advice: If you'd rather have soft shells heat corn of flour tortillas on a skillet for best results.
