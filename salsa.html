Salsa

Both a great snack, and something fun to pair with tacos!

Ingredients

  • 4 tomatoes
  • 2 jalepenos
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 bunch of cilantro
  • 1 tbsp of salt

Instructions

  1. Chop up the tomatoes, jalepenos, onion, and cilantro and add to the blender
  2. Juice the lemon into the blender
  3. Add salt to the blender
  4. Blend until liquidy
  5. Serve with chips!
