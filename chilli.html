Easy Chilli

Meat and chilli? What could go wrong

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • McCormick Season or Cajun Spice
  • Oil
  • Bell Pepper
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Chilli Powder

Instructions

  1. Season beef
  2. Brown beef
  3. Prinkle Chilli Powder till dark red
  4. Cook till ready, add water as needed
