Breakfast for Dinner

For when you're too lazy to really cook, but still want to have a complete meal

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 2 pancakes
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • maple syrup
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the bacon slices
  2. Cook the eggs
  3. Add salt and pepper to taste
  4. Make the pancakes
  5. Add maple syrup and butter to pancakes
