About the Chef

Hey everyone! If you love food in any capacity, you've come to the right place.

This website was born out of my need to quickly come up with a deliverable for the H4I Bootcamp.

Backstory

Below I will go into detail about the genesis of this site.

My Inspiration

In high school, I spent six months living abroad as a foreign exchange student in Australia. My host mother was kind enough to offer to make me lunch every day. Always a people pleaser, when she asked me what I wanted her to make, I told her whatever was easiest.

I will always rue that day.

From that day forward, she started making me lettuce salads for lunch. Sometimes there were cucumbers, sometimes not. But basically it was just a pile of lettuce with no dressing. That's when I knew I had to start cooking for myself.

Now

Now, as a college student, I understand my host mom's struggle. I am short on both money and time, so convinience is my top priority. I have figured out how to make my own food, and I am choosing to share some recipes on this website.
