Peanut Butter and Jelly

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 2 teaspoons berry jam

Instructions

  1. Take out all the ingredients, a plate, and a butter knife
  2. Place the bread slices face up
  3. Spread the peanut butter evenly over one slice of bread
  4. Spread the jam evenly on top of the peanut butter
  5. Place the other bread slice on top
  6. Cut the sandwich diagonally in half if you are feeling fancy
  7. Enjoy the peanut butter and jelly sandwich! Perhaps even take it with you on a hike!
