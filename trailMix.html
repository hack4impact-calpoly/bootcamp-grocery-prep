Trail Mix

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup cashews
  • 0.5 cup almonds
  • 0.5 cup peanuts
  • 0.5 cup raisins

Instructions

  1. Take out all the ingredients and big jar
  2. Add the cashews, almonds, peanuts, raisins to a jar
  3. Close the lid
  4. Shake the jar aggressively up and down until everything is mixed
  5. Enjoy the trail mix! Perhaps even take it on a picnic!
