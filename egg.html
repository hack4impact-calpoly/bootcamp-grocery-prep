Szechwan Eggplant

Spicy and delicious

Ingredients

  • eggplant
  • oil
  • soy sauce
  • vinegar
  • sugar
  • ginger minced
  • garlic minced
  • ground pork
  • red peppers

Instructions

  1. Cut eggplant into 2-inch strips
  2. In a wok or skillet, head oil and fry eggplant till soft. Remove oil and set aside
  3. Pour out oil, fry meat and stir in eggplant
  4. Combine, stir, enjoy!
