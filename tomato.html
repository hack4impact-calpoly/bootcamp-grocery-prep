Tomato Egg

Fluffy, soft, juicy, it's a match made in heaven.

Serving Size:

1

Ingredients

  • 2Eggs
  • 1tbs oil
  • 1scallions
  • 1tbs sugar
  • 2Tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Beat eggs with salt
  2. Heat oil in pan
  3. Add tomato, scallion and cook till juices are released
  4. Add egg and season
