Rose Black Tea

Black tea, but with the sweet rose aroma.

Ingrediends

  • 1 spoon of black tea leaves
  • 1/2 spoon of rose petals
  • 1 cup water

Instructions

  1. Boil your water
  2. Pour the water over your tea leaves and rose petals and into your cup
  3. Enjoy!
